Vegetarian burger

If meat isn’t your food of choice, don’t despair – veggie and bean burgers are more delicious than they have ever been! Just because these burgers lack meat, that absolutely does not mean that they lack flavor. Fresh beans are a great substitution to meat in a burger patty. Not only do they pack quite a bit of protein, but when prepared correctly their texture is meaty enough to come pretty close to the texture of a meat based burger. (They also taste much better than frozen veggie burgers!) Beans aren’t the only ingredient to use when making veggies burgers. With the abundance of fresh produce at this time of year, you should take full advantage of it. Some other ingredients that work well include corn, lentils, onion. Portobello mushrooms are also a favorite because their texture is meaty, like a beef burger would be
